The Definition

PepHands: A twitch emote used at the end of a phrase to indicate sadness and/or depression or to indicate the prior phrase was indicative of that. source: Urban Dictionary

Example Sentences:

"I can't believe I still play *game*, I have no life PepeHands."

"That was the worst luck I've ever had PepeHands"
